# Excel-Capstone-Project
Capstone project analyzing E‑commerce sales trends and profitability using Excel functions, pivot tables, and visualizations.

# 🛒 E‑commerce Sales Analysis (Excel Capstone Project)

**Tools:** Microsoft Excel | Lookup Functions | Pivot Tables | Charts & Dashboards  
**Domain:** Retail | Customer Analytics  

---

## 📊 Project Overview
This project explores E‑commerce sales data to uncover insights into:
- Regional and category‑wise performance
- Profitability trends across product segments
- Customer purchase behavior and repeat orders
- Seasonal demand and quarterly growth patterns

The analysis was performed entirely in **Excel**, leveraging formulas, pivot tables, and interactive dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
1. Identify top‑performing product categories and regions.  
2. Analyze profit margins and discount impact.  
3. Detect underperforming segments and suggest improvements.  
4. Study customer purchase frequency and loyalty.  
5. Provide clear Excel dashboards for decision‑making.  

---

## 📂 Dataset & Attributes
- **Order ID** – Unique transaction identifier  
- **Customer Name** – Buyer details  
- **Region** – Geographic segmentation  
- **Category & Sub‑Category** – Product classification  
- **Sales, Profit, Discount** – Financial metrics  
- **Order Date, Ship Date** – Timeline attributes  

---

## 🔧 Excel Techniques Used
- **Lookup Functions (VLOOKUP, HLOOKUP, INDEX‑MATCH)** for mapping product and region details  
- **Pivot Tables** for aggregating sales and profit by category, region, and time  
- **Calculated Fields** for profit margin and discount impact  
- **Conditional Formatting** to highlight top/bottom performers  
- **Charts & Dashboards** (bar charts, line charts, pie charts, KPI cards) for visualization  

---

## 📊 Dashboards
- **Sales by Region & Category**  
- **Profitability Trends (2019–2025)**  
- **Customer Loyalty & Repeat Orders**  
- **Quarterly Growth & Seasonal Demand**  

---

## 🚀 Key Insights
- 📈 Technology & Furniture categories drive maximum revenue.  
- 💰 West & East regions show highest profitability.  
- 🔄 Repeat customers contribute significantly to long‑term growth.  
- 🛒 Seasonal spikes observed in Q4 sales across all categories.  
- 🔻 Discounts beyond 20% reduce profit margins drastically.  

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This Excel‑based capstone project demonstrates how **lookup functions, pivot tables, and dashboards** can transform raw data into actionable business insights.  
The findings highlight the importance of **regional focus, discount optimization, and customer loyalty programs** in driving sustainable growth.
